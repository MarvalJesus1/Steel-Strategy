Guns

  • The AK-47 is one of the most widely used assault rifles in the world.
  • The M16 is the standard service rifle for the U.S. military.
  • The Barrett M82 is a famous .50 caliber sniper rifle.

Tanks

  • The German Tiger I tank was feared during World War II for its heavy armor and firepower.
  • The American M1 Abrams is known for its advanced armor and mobility.
  • The Russian T-34 was one of the most effective, efficient, and influential tank designs of WWII.

Aircraft

  • The F-22 Raptor is a stealth fighter used by the U.S. Air Force.
  • The Spitfire was a key fighter aircraft for the UK in WWII.
  • The B-52 Stratofortress has been in service since the 1950s and is still used today.

Flags

  • The red flag is often used as a symbol of revolution or warning in military contexts.
  • The white flag universally signals surrender.
  • Many military branches have unique flags distinct from their national flags.
